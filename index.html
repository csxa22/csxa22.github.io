Your Trusted Partner in Metal Packaging

Garg Industries is a trusted name in precision-engineered packaging for India's food and beverage & the aerosol industry.

Founded in 2015 in the foothills of Kumaon, Uttarakhand, our state-of-the-art facility at SIDCUL Pantnagar combines cutting-edge technology with a decade of packaging expertise to deliver superior quality products.

Today, we serve markets across North India with a reputation built on reliability, quality, and exceptional service—ensuring timely delivery at competitive prices.
