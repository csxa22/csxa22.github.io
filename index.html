About Us

GargCans India Pvt Ltd is first time initiative in India to manufacture easy open ends of both Aluminium and Tin plate as per international standards. The state-of-the-art facility has commissioned in Greater Noida and started production of tin and aluminium easy open ends. The company is comprised of highly experienced metal cans & easy openends manufacturing team and believes in high quality standard which is only way to success. You are welcome to explore opportunities with us!!
